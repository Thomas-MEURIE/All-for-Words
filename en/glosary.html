Lexique

Feminism:

To be a feminist is to think that women and men deserve the same rights and the same opportunities. There are different movements and different feminist currents, but the basis is the same: gender equality, equal pay, equal freedom of expression, equal safety in the streets etc. Actually, social, political and economic equality.

Patriarchy:

Hierarchical society in which men hold more power (patriarchal society).

Sexism:

See women as inferior to men.

Misogyny:

Hate of women.

Sorority:

Represents female solidarity, mutual help between women.
