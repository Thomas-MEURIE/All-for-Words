Women and their money

Women couldn't manage their money for quite a while. Indeed, they had to wait for certain dates to manage it without their husbands or fathers.

Money management

The beginning of the financial independence of women only began in 1907. Indeed, it is from this year that women recover their wages. Before that, it was their husbands who collected them. In the same year, women can now sit on the industrial tribunal. This is counsel aimed to settling disputes between employers and employees.
To continue, in 1965, they were able to open a bank account for them, without the authorization of their spouses. They can therefore manage their own money without someone behind and without having to share it.

Education for women

In 1938, they could finally go to university, and obtain an identity document (card, passport, etc.).
Then, from 1967, women can finally benefit from the scholarship of Paris. This was beneficial for women with low resources to be able to study in large schools.

Women and companies

In 1983, the Roudy law was put in place, allowing women to have the same professional equality as men. In 2012, the French State will publish a decree on the implementation of companies' obligations for this professional equality. Companies that do not respect this equality are now more severely punished. Finally in 2013, the national inter‐professional agreement would like professional equality both in the quality of life at work and in salary, which allows women to be heard as well as men at their work.

To conclude, women therefore have a constant improvement in their financial life, going through work, studies and management. But there is still a lot of gender inequality, there are still a lot of companies that do not respect this equality in the world, and some countries are turning a blind eye to this.

Thomas MEURIE, October 10, 2021
